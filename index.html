The Chef

A passion for food. A love for connection.

Food tastes better when it's personal.

After years of working in high-end restaurant kitchens, I realized something was missing. The food was beautiful, the technique was flawless, but the connection with the people eating the food was completely lost behind swinging kitchen doors.

I started Doorstep & Dine because I wanted to bring the rigor and quality of fine dining directly to the people enjoying it. I wanted to see their faces when they took the first bite. I wanted to answer their questions about how a sauce was made.

"I don't just want to be your caterer. I want to be your private chef, your culinary guide, and your friend in the kitchen."

When you book an experience with me, you're getting more than just a meal. You're getting an evening of storytelling, culinary education, and a completely stress-free dining experience in the comfort of your own home.

From sourcing the best local ingredients to leaving your kitchen cleaner than I found it, every detail is considered. Let's create something beautiful together.
